My favourite orientation memory...
  • Have you ever thought how life in an IIT is? Have you been through all the websites that speak about IITs but still haven't got the real feel? Have you always wondered how life becomes once you get into a college? Are you curious if an engineering college is exactly similar to what they show you on '3 Idiots'?
  • When I was in the phase of choosing a college, I had millions of questions in my mind, which weren't answered by the webpages I visited. I was frustrated and nervous. Everybody wrote about academic programs and other official things but nobody wrote about the basic things that students would face once they get there.
  • I am a student of IIT Kanpur, who has just experienced the 1st year of college life - IIT life. Here, I have tried my best to elaborate on certain things, answer some unanswered questions and provide an insight of a life of a fresher girl in IIT. I haven't written about official things because you can easily get them on the official website of IIT Kanpur. My purpose is to make you aware of IIT Kanpur as seen through my eyes, someone who never knew how college life would be.
    I hope you would like it!
